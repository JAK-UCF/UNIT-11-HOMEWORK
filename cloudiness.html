Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

The reported data for cloudiness intimates that although the data is collected in units of percentage, many of the weather stations are reporting the percentage equivalent to the meteorological standard unit of an okta (a scale of 9 units ranging from 0 to 8).

This, "okta effect" as it could be called, results in heavy clustering of data points along certain percentage lines.

As can be seen in the displayed data points, not all weather stations report in the standardized okta unit. However, the reporting methods for cloudiness are unknown for the stations used in this data set.
